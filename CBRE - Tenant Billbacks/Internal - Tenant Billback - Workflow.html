Current State vs. Outcome Automation
400 North Aberdeen, Chicago — Life Sciences Building — 15+ Lab Tenants
Today — Manual Process
Trigger

Monthly Billing Cycle

Tim decides it's time to run billing (beginning of each month). No automated trigger. Timing depends on when meter data is available and when Tim has time.

Inputs

Meter Data

CHW, HW, DW, compressed air, gas readings from the BAS. Electricity kWh from sub-meters (4 tenants). Tim collects and consolidates all readings.

Variable Utility Rates

ComEd electric, Nicor gas, city water rates. Tim checks if rates changed since last month.

Tenant Registry

15+ suites with design airflow %, CHW/HW loads, sub-meter flags. Embedded in the workbook.

Transformation

Tim Manually Enters Readings

Opens the HVAC Cost Workbook (603 columns). Inputs monthly meter readings into the "Tenant Submetered Usages" section for each tenant × utility type.

Tim Updates Rates (If Changed)

Manually updates rate cells in the spreadsheet. Elec: $0.072/kWh commodity, $15.36/kW demand. Gas: $1.13/therm. Water: $0.012/gal.

Excel Formulas Calculate Charges

Consumption × rate per utility. Plant costs allocated by design airflow %. Calculates per-tenant totals across 6 utility types.

Outputs

Manual Tenant Breakdown Documents

Tim creates tenant-specific documents from the spreadsheet results. Sends to the property management team, who handles actual invoicing.

Delivery to PM Team

Tim sends breakdown documents to the property management team. Manual process, no tracking.

No trend analysis. No anomaly detection.
Tim eyeballs month-over-month changes. Flags spikes by memory and experience only.

Discovery Questions for Client Call

C1 What BAS system is installed at 400 N Aberdeen? Can it export data automatically (API, SFTP, scheduled email)?
C2 How does Tim learn about utility rate changes from ComEd/Nicor? How often do they change?
C3 Is an admin fee applied to tenant billback charges? If so, what percentage?
C4 Can Tim share an example of the tenant breakdown document he currently sends to the property management team?
C5 Are there audit or compliance requirements for the billing documentation?
